Your closing steps
1
Earnest money deposit ▼

The earnest money deposit needs to be dropped off or delivered to the title company within 2 business days of your accepted offer date.

David will send the contract to your lender and order inspections right away, so things move quickly from day one.
Due by Apr 3, 2026 Deliver to title company
2
Work with your lender & appraisal ▼

This step is where you will do the most work during the transaction. Your lender will give you a checklist of documents they need for final loan approval. Get those over promptly and check your email daily since lenders move fast and missing a request can cause delays.

The lender will also order the appraisal, often before inspections are even done. The appraisal confirms the home's value supports the loan amount. You do not need to do anything for it, but it is happening in the background at the same time you are working on your loan docs. David will keep you updated on both.
Check email daily Respond to lender quickly Contingency: Apr 26
3
Inspections & repair negotiations ▼

After inspections are complete, David will help put together a repair request focused on safety items and major issues, not cosmetic things.

Here is how repair negotiations work:
  • The seller must be given the opportunity to make the repairs themselves. That is required by the contract.
  • However, we can also let the seller know you are open to a credit instead of repairs, which is often easier for everyone, especially since the seller is an investor.
  • It has to be one or the other, not both.
  • The seller can agree to all repairs, some repairs, offer a credit, or decline entirely.

If the outcome is not acceptable to you, you have the right to cancel the transaction and receive your full earnest money deposit back.

Deadline: Apr 16 Repairs or credit, not both Can cancel if needed
4
Final walkthrough ▼

Typically 1 to 2 days before closing, you and David will walk through the home together. The goal is to confirm the home is still in the same condition as when you made your offer and that no unexpected damage has occurred since the inspection.

This is your last chance to flag anything before you own it, so take your time and look around carefully.
Around Apr 28-29 David will schedule this
5
Signing & closing day ▼

You will sign your documents at the title company. Bring your driver's license.

Payment for your down payment and closing costs can be a cashier's check or a wire transfer. If you are wiring funds, we recommend doing it the day before closing since most bank wire cutoffs are around 1 to 2pm and a missed wire means a delayed closing.

Once the title company has your wire, your signed loan documents, and the lender funds the loan, everything is submitted to the county for recording. Once the county confirms the recording, you get the keys. That typically happens between 12pm and 5pm on a business day.
Closing: Apr 30, 2026 Bring driver's license Wire funds day before Keys after county records
